  • Dear Reader

    I'm Tucker Johnson, currently a senior here. This is my last year in the 'sheltered bubble' that is the public high school system. As I've counted the years going here, I've slowly began to notice the important parts of my life shift away from school. For this "portfolio" presentation, I want to focus on work relating to my future, where I'm heading as an adult. As such, the pieces on my website this year are entirely unrelated to my school work. Every day I believe more and more that the ways I'm growing, both personally and in my careers are linked to the world outside of high school. The projects I'm showing are what I think are the best examples of what I can do on my own, a situation I'll be facing all-to-soon. I'm experienced with giving my portfolio by this point, so feel free to ask anything that's on your mind. Since these projects arent just school assignments, it's harder for them to "speak for themselves". Other than exploring my body of work, I hope most of what you learn can come from our discussion.
