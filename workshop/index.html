Where can I show off my games?


https://itch.io - basically Steam for indies - free game hosting / marketplace centered on devs (super cool)

https://forums.tigsource.com/index.php?board=27.0 TIGsource forums - practically the only forum for making games left. Pretty active, and a decent place for keeping a log of your project’s progress. Also a great place to find new and interesting games.
